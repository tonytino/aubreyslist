- `[manual]` Added the transparent trust summary + recency roll-up (#29, ADR-007). A new pure, client-safe derivation module (`app/trust/summary.ts`) rolls a claim's visible `getClaimAggregate` evidence up into a render-ready summary — confirm/dispute counts, a coarse relative-time "last confirmed 3 weeks ago" recency phrase, and a "may be stale" flag past the (admin-tunable) 6-month staleness window — never a hidden score. A reusable `ClaimTrustSummaryRow` component renders one claim's roll-up (drop-in for the future browse list, #33), and `CommunityClaims` lists them into the listing-detail "Community claims" slot via a new batched `getListingClaimAggregates` loader (`app/server/attestations/listing-summary.ts`) that fetches every claim with its counts + recency in one query. The headline `SafetySummary` celiac-safe vs. gluten-friendly cue is now derived from the `celiac_safe_vs_gluten_friendly` claim's aggregate, staying honest by construction — no attestation evidence leaves the existing "Not yet attested" empty state, contested/disputed evidence falls back to the lower gluten-friendly claim, and an aged consensus is flagged stale rather than trusted, so a celiac is never shown a fabricated rating. Exhaustive unit tests cover counts formatting, relative-time/recency, staleness, and the headline derivation (incl. empty/no-evidence cases). Also exported the per-enum string-union types (`ClaimAttribute`, etc.) from `db/schema.ts`.
